1. Player enters room and calanedar pieces raise (animation in place)
2. Kira grabs the handle and rotates the outer ring into place.
3. Kira will activate switch. If ring properly placed, all rings will lower one step
4. Kira will need to find one of the remaining handles hidden in the room.
5. Once finding the handle, Kira will put it into place in the middle ring
6. Steps will repeat until entire calendar is flat.
7. Once center of calendar is lowered, exit door will unlock.
