The level will be pitch black except where fire lights the path.
The first Brazier will already have fire in place.
Player will need to push brazier to the first dangling rope, lighting rope and all items in Step1
Rope at end of Step1 will light 2nd brazier
Player moves next brazier around corner to set off Step2.
Repeat for Step 3. Once Step 3 is triggered, the spawner will engage.
After a certain number of ants, the exit will open.
